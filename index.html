The App automatically collects crash reports when it encounters an error or unexpectedly stops. Analytics Data

The App collects anonymized analytics data about how you interact with it — such as which buttons you tap, which settings you choose, and general usage patterns. This helps me understand how the App is used and improve the experience for everyone. This data does not identify you personally.

If you choose to contact me via the email option on the About page, you will be sharing your email address and any information you include in your message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

These providers are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

Crash report and analytics data are retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Emails you send me are retained for as long as needed to resolve your inquiry.
